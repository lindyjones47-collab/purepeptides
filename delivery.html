Delivery Information

Dispatch times, carriers, tracking, and worldwide shipping details.

Dispatch Times

Orders placed before 3 PM (Mon–Fri) are dispatched the same day. Orders placed after the cut-off, on weekends, or on public holidays are dispatched on the next business day — once payment has been received.

Shipping Carriers

We ship worldwide using trusted couriers:

  • DHL Express — fastest international option with full tracking.
  • Deutsche Post — economical European and international delivery.
  • Royal Mail — UK and international tracked services.

Estimated Delivery Times

  • UK: 1–2 business days
  • Europe: 2–5 business days
  • Rest of world: 5–12 business days

These are estimates; customs processing can affect international timeframes.

Tracking

You will receive tracking updates by email and SMS. You can also check progress anytime via our Check Order Status page.
