What is PT-141?

PT-141 (Bremelanotide) is a synthetic cyclic heptapeptide and a ligand of the melanocortin receptor family. Its sequence is Ac-Nle-cyclo[Asp-His-D-Phe-Arg-Trp-Lys]-OH. It is supplied as a lyophilised powder for laboratory research.

Handling PT-141 in research protocols
