Tracked Peptide Delivery Across the UK & EU

Published 27 January 2026 · PurePeptides Research Hub · For research use only.

Getting research peptides to the laboratory quickly and intact is part of the service. Here is how dispatch and delivery work at PurePeptides. For research use only.

Same-day dispatch

Orders placed before 3 PM on a business day (Mon-Fri) are dispatched the same day, so material is on its way without delay.

Tracked and insured

Shipments across the UK and Europe are sent with tracked, insured delivery so you can follow progress and have cover in transit. Packaging is discreet.

Why cold chain usually isn't needed

Peptides ship in lyophilised (freeze-dried) form, which is relatively stable and tolerates short ambient transit. Cold storage matters once the material reaches the lab — lyophilised vials at -20°C, reconstituted solutions at 2-8°C — rather than during shipping. See our delivery information for details.
