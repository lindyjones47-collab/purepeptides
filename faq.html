Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering research-grade peptides from PurePeptides.

Answers to the most common questions about our research peptides, ordering, payment, shipping, and storage. Can't find what you need? Message us on WhatsApp or email sales@europemeds.com.

Are your peptides safe for human use?⌄

No. All PurePeptides products are sold strictly for laboratory and in-vitro research purposes only. They are not intended for human or animal consumption, nor for any therapeutic, diagnostic, or clinical use.

What purity can I expect?⌄

Every peptide is manufactured in a state-of-the-art laboratory and tested to ensure a purity of greater than 99%. Certificates of Analysis (COA) can be requested by email for most products.

How are the peptides shipped and stored?⌄

Products ship in lyophilised (freeze-dried) form in sealed vials with discreet, secure packaging. We recommend storing lyophilised peptides in a freezer and reconstituted peptides refrigerated. See our Delivery Information page for details.

When will my order be dispatched?⌄

Orders placed before 3 PM (Mon–Fri) are dispatched the same day. Orders after the cut-off or on weekends are dispatched the next business day.

How do I pay for my order?⌄

After you place your order at checkout, our team will contact you by email or WhatsApp with the bank payment details required to complete your purchase. Your order is processed once payment has been received.

Which countries do you ship to?⌄

We ship worldwide via DHL Express, Deutsche Post, and Royal Mail. Delivery timeframes vary by destination — see Delivery Information.

Can I track my order?⌄

Yes. You receive tracking updates by email and SMS, and you can check progress anytime on our Check Order Status page.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?⌄

Yes. For bulk and wholesale enquiries, contact us at sales@europemeds.com or via WhatsApp at +33 7 58 56 35 17.

How do I reconstitute a peptide?⌄

Use bacteriostatic water and our Peptide Calculator Tool to determine the correct volume for your desired concentration. Always follow proper laboratory handling procedures.

What is your returns policy?⌄

Due to the sensitive nature of research chemicals, we generally cannot accept returns once an order has shipped. See our Returns page for full details and exceptions.
