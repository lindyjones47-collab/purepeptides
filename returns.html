Returns & Refunds Policy

Because our products are research chemicals whose integrity depends on strict storage and handling, we are generally unable to accept returns once an order has been dispatched. This protects all customers by ensuring no product that has left our controlled environment is ever resold.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged, or you received an incorrect item, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with your order number and photographs of the issue. We will investigate and, where appropriate, arrange a replacement or refund.

Non-Returnable Circumstances
